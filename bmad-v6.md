## What is BMAD v6?

### Simple Explanation
Think of BMAD v4 as having separate toolboxes for different jobs (one for software, one for games, one for writing). BMAD v6 is like having one master toolbox (BMad Core) that can hold any specialized tools you need, and you can even build your own custom tools that fit perfectly.

### Technical Explanation
BMAD v6 introduces **BMad Core** - a universal framework for human-AI collaboration that enables:
- **Modular Architecture**: Core framework + specialized modules
- **Custom Content Creation**: Build your own agents, workflows, and modules
- **Scale-Adaptive Intelligence**: Automatically adjusts complexity based on project needs
- **Universal Compatibility**: Works across all major IDEs and AI platforms
